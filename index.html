About the Author

Classified

Jordan Hayes is a storyteller drawn to the edges of reality—where memory blurs, time bends, and the ordinary turns strange. Raised between two continents and never quite at home in either, Jordan writes from the friction of displacement, weaving quiet character studies into speculative worlds. Pandora's Madness is the first book in the Veil Cycle trilogy.

Preview

The last thing Elias remembered was the rain. Not the sound of it, but the shape of it—each drop a needle of static against his skin. When he woke beneath a sky the color of old television static, the world had already begun to forget him.

In a city where doors lead to places that don't exist on any map, Elias must piece together the fragments of a life he no longer recognizes—and confront the figure waiting at the heart of the labyrinth: a version of himself who never left.

Sera Nyx

The Echo

A woman made of static and second chances.

Age: Unknown (appears 27)

Affiliation: The Resistance of Glimpses

Ability: Echo-step — can rewind her own timeline by up to eleven seconds, leaving afterimages called "echoes" in her wake.

Sera is what happens when a person rewinds too many times: fragmented, fierce, and running out of rewinds. She remembers versions of Elias that he has forgotten—and is desperate to keep him from finding the truth at the center of the maze.
